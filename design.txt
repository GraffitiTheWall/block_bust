name: block breaker.
objective: a moving line on the bottom of the screen will be given a ball that will bounce up and down, at random directions, to
hit a block. the player must hit all of the blocks to win.

images: 
1. the block.
2. the breaker.
3. and the ball.

powerups: 
1. 2 balls firing at once.
2. lengthen the width of the breaker. 
3. gain back a life.

set backs:
4. shorten the width of the breaker.
5. more blocks added.
6. subtract a life.
